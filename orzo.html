Orzo Pasta

ASIAN ORZO - 16 OZ.

Flavors: Cayenne Pepper, Chive, Garlic Parsley, Lemon Ginger, & Sesame A daring combination of forward-flavors that will thrill your taste-buds! This orzo was created as a “sister” to our Pacific Rim Blend, and was intended as a great complement to Asian stir-fry dishes.

CUBANO ORZO - 16 OZ.

Flavors: Black Bean, Cilantro Lime, Plain Semolina & Sweet Red Pepper The traditional Cuban flavors of this orzo create an absolutely vibrant plate. This pasta is just as appealing to the eyes as it is to the taste buds

LEMON GARLIC ORZO - 16 OZ.

This Mediterranean-inspired orzo makes the perfect accompaniment to grilled fish or chicken. We originally created this orzo at the request of one of our food service customers.

RAINBOW ORZO - 16 OZ.

Flavors: Plain Semolina, Spinach, & Sweet Red Pepper This is a colorful, garden-fresh product that can be served year-round, either hot or cold. Mix it with diced garden vegetables, a hint of fresh basil, and vinaigrette dressing to create a delicious side dish for beef, chicken, or veal. We created this orzo in Italian colors for one of our Denver caterers.

SOUTHWEST ORZO - 16 OZ.

Flavors: Black Bean, Indian Corn Maize, & Red Southwest Chile These flavors are blended to result in a subtle, yet spicy, Southwestern-style pasta that retains its intense flavor when served either hot or cold.

SPICY RED HOT THAI CURRY ORZO - 16 OZ.

Add international flare to a weeknight gourmet meal. This vibrant Thai dish is both fragrant and delicious, an absolute crowd pleaser for those in search of spicy pasta.

SPRING MEDLEY ORZO - 16 OZ.

This product is seasonal! Available from March - July. Flavors: Chive, Lemon Pepper, & Parsley This orzo has bright full flavors that marry perfectly with a nice piece of fresh fish or grilled meat. Our founding creator, Executive Chef David Bowen, was inspired to create this orzo after tasting a certain Rice-a-Roni dish in the 90’s.

SUPREME ORZO - 16 OZ.

Flavors: Fire-Roasted Red Pepper, Porcini Mushroom, & Saffron. Concentrated and intense is what this orzo is all about! If you like succulent, deep flavors, this is the orzo for you. The marriage of these flavors was originally requested by a local Denver chef. He certainly knew what he was doing as this has become a favorite Farmers’ market orzo.

SWEET POTATO ORZO - 16 OZ.

If you like sweet potato, you'll love this orzo!
